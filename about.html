Bio

Full-stack Web Developer and certified ScrumMaster® with experience as a project manager for technology companies. My background in consulting and FinTech provides a unique perspective on how customers, companies, and other developers interact with digital products. I enjoy breaking down complex problems and building processes to solve them which led me to a deeper involvement in building meaningful web applications.


In order to make my passion a reality, I completed the Full-stack bootcamp through the University of Kansas where I expanded my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js. and MySQL. In 2018, I re-located back home to Kansas City from the San Francisco Bay Area where I spent the past five years partnering with technology executives to deliver on their strategic initiatives.


In my personal time, I enjoy re-watching Game of Thrones with a glass of wine or two with my husband and often found cuddling with my two fur-babies, Cooper and Grizz. I also like to listen to true-crime podcasts and re-read Queen J.K. Rowling’s Harry Potter series. I have a passion for astronomy and the outdoors including camping, hiking, and fishing. Fun fact: my husband and I hiked a section of the Pacific Crest Trail, logging 280 miles in 18 days.
